SDU online meeting app

An online meeting app designed for Shandong University

This was an assignment for one of my undergraduate courses. The instructor's requirement was to design an app interface for online meetings specifically for my university. At the time, China was under pandemic lockdown, and online meetings served as the primary learning channel for us students.
