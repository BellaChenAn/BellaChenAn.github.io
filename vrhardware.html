A VR Device Designed For Children

This is a part of my undergraduate final project. Most current VR headsets are designed with adults in mind, making them heavy and uncomfortable for children. However, recent data shows that children and teenagers are among the primary users of VR devices. This design is based on child ergonomics and psychology to create a VR device specifically tailored for children.
