Lovestory of liangzhu

Cultural and Creative Design Inspired by the Ancient Chinese Love Story 'Liang Zhu' (CI Design)

"Liang Zhu" is an ancient Chinese legend that tells the love story of Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai. Due to family obstacles, they could not marry, and ultimately, they transformed into butterflies, becoming an eternal symbol of love. Based on this romantic story, I designed a complete set of cultural and creative products with a romantic theme through the analysis of traditional Chinese butterfly patterns.

These characters were created using MidJourney. This step aims to explore the value of AI co-design
