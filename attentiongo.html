AttentionGo

A Tangible Attention Training Toy For Children

Based on my previous research, it has been demonstrated that tangible BCI (Brain-Computer Interface) games activate the human brain more effectively than video games, leading to better therapeutic outcomes. Therefore, I designed a tangible toy for training children's attention. Through modular assembly and combination, the toy stimulates children's motivation to engage in training.
