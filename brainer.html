VR-BCI Attention Training Game For ADHD Children

BCI-NF (Brain-Computer Interface Neurofeedback) is a method of attention training for children with ADHD that has gained popularity due to its non-invasive nature and lack of side effects. Traditional BCI-NF games are often monotonous, but the immersive experience provided by VR (Virtual Reality) technology can significantly enhance the training experience for children. This design is based on theories related to game therapy and psychological intervention and uses Unity to develop a VR game.
